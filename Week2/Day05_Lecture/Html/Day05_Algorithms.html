Sorting Algorithms

Performance

When talking about the performance of our algorithms in code, we use the order-of notation sometimes called the Big-O notation.
Time Complexity

When discussing the performance of the sorting algorithms, you want to consider the worst-case performance. How long does it take to sort the list in the worst-case scenario? Not all sorting algorithms perform the same. Some are better in different scenarios than others. Consider the usage of the algorithms to see which is better for your application.
